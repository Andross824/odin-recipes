First, you want to start off by acquiring a good lasagna pasta. You can search online for which store breands are best, or you could make your owm at home!

With that out of the way, you need to cook the lasagna, while that is going you will want to grab a deep baking dish and layer it with some ricota cheese.

Then you layer some pasta over it. On top of the first layer of pasta you will add your ragu, a layer of ricotta, and pecorino or romano cheese grated fine.

Add another layer of pasta and the like, until you reach the top of the dish or run out of pasta. Top the final layer with some fresh mozzarella before the grated cheese.

Bake this in a preheated oven at 400 degrees F, for about 40 minutes or until the top is almost golden brown in color. Voila! homemade Lasagna for the family!
